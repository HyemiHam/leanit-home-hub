## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal information in the following ways:

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Registration**: Name, email address, phone number, password, and profile information

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Social Media Integration**: If you connect your social media account, we receive profile information you authorize us to access
- **Business Partners**: Information shared by partners for service provision or marketing purposes

- **Korea**: If you are under 13 years old, we require parental/guardian consent before collecting your information

## 4. DATA RETENTION

### 4.1 Korea (PIPA)
- Personal information is retained **only as long as necessary** to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
- Upon expiration of the retention period, information is **securely deleted or anonymized**
- Specific retention periods by data type:
  - **Account Information**: Duration of account + 3 years after account deletion (legal/tax purposes)
  - **Transaction Records**: 5 years (Korea's tax law requirement)
  - **Customer Service Records**: 3 years from last interaction
  - **Marketing Communications**: Until consent is withdrawn

### 4.2 United States (CCPA)
- We retain personal information for **the minimum time necessary** to provide Services
- Data retained longer than 12 months may be subject to your right to access historical data back to January 1, 2022
- Specific retention periods:
  - **Account Information**: Duration of account + 1 year
  - **Transaction Records**: 7 years (US tax/regulatory requirement)
  - **Log Data and Cookies**: 12-24 months
  - **Marketing Data**: Until opt-out request is processed

### 4.3 Data Deletion
You can request deletion of your personal information by contacting our Privacy Officer. We will comply with deletion requests within **10 days (Korea)** or **45 days (US)**, subject to legal retention requirements.

## 7. DATA SECURITY

### 7.1 Security Measures
We implement technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information:
- **Encryption**: Data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to authorized personnel only
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication for account access
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Employee Training**: Privacy and security awareness programs
- **Incident Response**: Documented procedures for data breach response

### 7.2 Data Breach Notification

**Korea (PIPA) - 72-Hour Notification Rule**
- We notify affected individuals **within 72 hours** of discovering a data breach
- Notification must include:
  - Categories of information compromised
  - Time/circumstances of breach
  - Measures to minimize damage
  - Countermeasures and remedial actions
  - Contact information for damage reports
- Notify PIPC **within 72 hours** if:
  - 1,000+ data subjects affected
  - Sensitive information compromised
  - Unique identification information compromised
  - Unauthorized external access occurred

**US - State-Specific Notification Laws**
- We comply with all state data breach notification laws
- Notification timeline varies by state (typically 30-60 days from discovery)
- Notification includes:
  - Description of breach
  - Types of information involved
  - Steps to mitigate damage
  - Contact information for inquiries
