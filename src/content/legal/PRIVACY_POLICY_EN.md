## 7. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

The privacy rights available to you depend on your location. Below are rights under major privacy regulations:

### 7.1 Rights Under CCPA/CPRA (California Residents)

You have the right to:
- **Know**: Request what personal information we collect and how we use it
- **Delete**: Request deletion of personal information (with exceptions)
- **Opt-Out**: Opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information
- **Correct**: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
- **Access**: Receive your information in a portable, usable format
- **Non-Discrimination**: Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

**How to Submit Requests:**
- Email: privacy@leanit.com
- Web Form: https://www.leanit.com/privacy-requests
- Mail: LEANIT Privacy Team, [Company Address]

We will respond within 45 days of a verifiable request. We may request additional information to verify your identity.

**California Consumer Law Rights:**
- Right to know the categories and sources of personal information collected
- Right to know the business purpose for collection
- Right to know the categories of third parties with whom information is shared
- Right to deletion of personal information collected
- Right to opt-out of sale or sharing of personal information
- Right to correct inaccurate personal information
- Right to limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information
- Right to appeal our decision on requests

## 8. Data Breach Notification

If we discover a security breach affecting your personal information, we will:
1. Investigate the breach promptly
2. Notify affected individuals without unreasonable delay
3. Provide details about the breach and measures taken
4. Comply with notification timelines required by law (typically 72 hours in GDPR jurisdictions, specific timeframes for CCPA/PIPA)
5. Cooperate with regulatory authorities as required

**Notice will include:**
- Description of the breach
- Categories of information involved
- Steps taken to secure information
- Recommended protective measures
- Contact information for inquiries
- Information about available remedies
